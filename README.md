# Pig Dodger Game

## Overview
Pig Dodge is a fun and engaging mobile game where players take on the role of a pig on a mission to avoid as many blocks of wood as possible.

## Features
- 🎮 **Addictive Gameplay**: Simple controls with challenging levels.
- 📈 **Leaderboard**: Compete with your high scores.

## Installation
Download the game [here](https://github.com/nathansynniang-debug/Pig-Dodge/releases/download/game/PigDodge.apk) and install

## How to Play
Tap in the left or right side of the screen to move to its corresponding direction

Avoid obstacles to increase your score.

Good luck
